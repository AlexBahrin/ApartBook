How Discount Periods Work

Discount periods automatically apply a percentage discount to bookings that overlap with the specified date range.

Proportional discounts: If a booking only partially overlaps with a discount period, the discount is applied only to the overlapping days.

Example: A 20% discount for Jan 1-10, with a booking from Jan 5-15, would apply 20% off to Jan 5-10 (6 nights) and regular price for Jan 11-15 (5 nights).
